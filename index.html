ABOUT

Ray Ukuame (he/they) is a twenty-year-old full-time author and poet from Nigeria. He mostly writes novels, poems, and short fiction in magazines like Green Black Tales.
He’s neurodivergent and bisexual. They dedicate their time to telling stories exploring mental health, sexuality, and other underrepresented topics in Nigerian Literature, often in YA and through science fiction. When not writing, you can find him binging YouTube videos on history, reading, or listening to full albums like the olden days of the 1980s.
